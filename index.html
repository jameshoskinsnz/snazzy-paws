Snazzy Paws Grooming was founded by a local Hamilton couple with one simple mission: to treat every dog in our community like one of our own. What started as a passion project has grown into a professional salon powered by a dedicated full-time team.





Why Choose Snazzy Paws?

  • Local & Dedicated: Based right here in the heart of the Waikato.
  • Professional Team: Expertly staffed by passionate animal lovers.
  • Full-Service Care: From nail trims to specialized spa treatments.
  • Convenient Hours: Open Monday–Saturday, 9 am to 5 pm.

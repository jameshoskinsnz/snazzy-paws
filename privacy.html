Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Booking or Contact forms, we collect personal details necessary to provide our dog grooming services. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and information about your pet (such as their name, breed, and age).

2. How We Use Your Data

The information you submit is used solely to manage your appointments, coordinate grooming schedules, and respond directly to your queries. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Confirm and schedule grooming appointments via our Dashboard.
  • Reach out to owners if scheduling changes or emergencies occur during a service.
  • Keep accurate records of specific breed requirements and medical requests.

3. Data Retention and Security

We prioritize the security of your details. Your personal submission history is stored securely and is strictly accessed only by authorized salon workers checking daily shifts. We never distribute, sell, or rent customer information to third-party marketing companies.

4. Your Rights

Under New Zealand privacy regulations, you have full rights to request access to any personal information we hold about you, or to ask that your client files be entirely removed from our database. If you wish to update or clear your files, please reach out to us using our Contact portal.
